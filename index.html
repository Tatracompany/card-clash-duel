How To Play

  • Two players join the same room or use quick match.
  • Each hand starts with 5 cards per player and bidding from 5 to 9 or pass.
  • The bid winner chooses trump, both players discard 3 cards, and both use the special draw phase.
  • Hands refill to 9 cards, then all 9 tricks are played.
  • If the contractor makes the bid, they score that many match points.
  • The match continues with new hands until one player reaches 21 points first.
